Before We Start

  1. Make sure your camera is supported by Cascable.
  2. Check that your camera's firmware is up-to-date.
  3. Check that Cascable is up-to-date.

Connecting Cascable to Your WiFi-Enabled Camera

  1. Press the Menu button on your camera and navigate to Wi-Fi in theSetup menu.
  2. Select Network connection within the Wi-Fi menu and choose Enable.
  3. Select Network settings > View SSID to view the name of the WiFi network created by your camera.
  4. Open Settings on your iOS device and navigate to Wi-Fi, then connect to the WiFi network displayed on your camera.
  5. Launch Cascable on your iOS device. It should automatically detect and connect to your camera.

Connecting Cascable to Your Camera Using a WU-1a/b WiFi Adapter

  1. Connect the WU-1a/b WiFi adapter to your camera and switch your camera on.
  2. Open Settings on your iOS device and navigate to Wi-Fi, then connect to the WiFi network being created by the WU-1a/b WiFi adapter.
  3. Launch Cascable on your iOS device. It should automatically detect and connect to your camera.
