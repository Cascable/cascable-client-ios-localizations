Cascable ★Pro

Cascable’s Pro packs unlock the potential of what you and your WiFi-enabled camera can achieve together.

How To Purchase

Head to Cascable’s in-app store, available from the Settings menu or by tapping on any of the features with a ★Pro icon.

From the Cascable Store, tap the Buy button and enter your Apple ID credentials or Touch ID.


How To Restore Purchases

If you’ve already purchased one or more Cascable Pro packs and would like to restore them to another device that’s using the same Apple ID, head to the Cascable Store and tap the Restore Purchases button at the bottom of the page.

Full Bundle

The Full Bundle is the most cost-effective way to unlock everything Cascable has to offer and includes everything from the Pro packs listed below at a savings over purchasing them individually.

Remote Control

The Remote Control pack unlocks advanced features for remote controlling your camera, including:

  • Instant Shot Review
    • A hi-res preview that pops up straight after taking a photo so that you can make sure you’ve got the shot you were after.
  • Focus Peaking
    • A red highlight area is drawn around areas of the live viewfinder image which have sharp edges, helping you to nail the focus of your shots.
  • Shutter Robot
    • Your friendly shutter automation assistant, with Shutter Robot you can schedule an intervalometer to shoot time-lapse scenes; set up exposure bracketing to shoot HDR; configure a bulb timer to take multi-minute exposures, such as when shooting at night or with filters; or just enable a simple self-timer.

Photo Management

The Photo Management pack unlocks advanced features for working with your photos, including:

  • On-Camera Image Preview
    • See hi-res previews of the photos on your camera.
  • RAW Support
    • Add support for previewing, downloading and sharing the RAW files from your camera. Whether you shoot Canon (CR2), Nikon (NEF), Olympus (ORF) or Sony (ARW) RAW, Cascable can handle them.
  • Filtering & Searching
    • Filter the photos downloaded to your iPhone or iPad by type, by date range, or use a free text search box to find shots taken using a specific camera make, model, lens, filename and more.

Night Mode

The Night Mode pack unlocks an app-wide dark theme, greatly enhancing night and low-light work. By dimming all of Cascable’s user interface your content is king, preserving your night vision so that you can focus on composing and taking nightscapes.
