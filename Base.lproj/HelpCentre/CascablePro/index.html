Full Bundle

The Full Bundle is the most cost-effective way to unlock everything Cascable has to offer, it includes everything from the Pro feature packs listed below at a savings over purchasing them individually.

Remote Control

The Remote Control pack unlocks advanced features for remote controlling your camera, including:

  • Instant Shot Review
    • A hi-res preview that pops up straight after taking the shot so that you can make sure you’ve got the shot your were after.
  • Focus Peaking
    • A red highlighted area is drawn around areas of the live viewfinder image which have sharp edges, helping you to nail the focus.
  • Shutter Robot
    • Your friendly shutter automation assistant, you can schedule an intervalometer to shoot time-lapse scenes; set up exposure bracketing to shoot HDR; configure a bulb timer to take multi-minute exposures, such as when shooting at night or with filters; or just enable a simple self-timer.

Photo Management

The Photo Management pack unlocks advanced features for working with your photos, including:

  • On-Camera Image Preview
    • See a hi-res preview of the photos on your WiFi-connected camera.
  • RAW Support
    • Add support for wireless previewing, downloading and shareing the original Canon (CR2), Nikon (NEF), Olympus (ORF) and Sony (ARW) RAW files directly from your camera.
  • Filtering & Searching
    • Filter the photos downloaded to your iPhone or iPad by type, RAW or JPEG, by date range, or use a free text search box to find shots taken using a specific camera make, model, lens, filename and more.

Night Mode

The Night Mode pack unlocks an app-wide dark theme, greatly enhancing night and low-light work. By dimming all of Cascable’s gorgeous user interface, your content is king. By preserving your night vision you can focus on composing and taking nightscapes.
