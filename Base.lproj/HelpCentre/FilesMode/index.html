Files Mode displays the photos on the connected camera as well as any photos downloaded into Cascable.

To download a photo from a connected camera, tap the button next the the photo's name. Once the photo has finished downloading, it will be available to view even when the camera is disconnected.

To delete one or more downloaded photos, tap the "Select" button and tap the desired photos. Then, tap the button and confirm the deletion. The chosen photos will no longer be available to view.

To view a downloaded photo, tap the photo's thumbnail. In the full-screen view that appears, you can swipe left and right to switch between downloaded photos.
