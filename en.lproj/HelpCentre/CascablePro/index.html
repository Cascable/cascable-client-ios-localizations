Cascable Pro unlocks the potential of what you and your WiFi-enabled camera can achieve together.

How To Purchase

Head to Cascable’s in-app store, available from the Purchases section of Cascable’s settings or by tapping on any of the features with a ★PRO icon.

From the Cascable Store, tap the Buy button and enter your Apple ID credentials or Touch ID.


How To Restore Purchases

If you’ve already purchased Cascable Pro and would like to restore it to another device that’s using the same Apple ID, head to the Purchases section of Cascable’s settings and tap the Restore Purchases button.

Unlocked Features

When purchasing or subscribing to Cascable Pro, you get access to many powerful new features.

Remote Control

The remote control section of the app gains advanced features for remote controlling your camera, including:

  • Instant Shot Review
    • A hi-res preview that pops up straight after taking a photo so that you can make sure you’ve got the shot you were after.
  • Focus Peaking
    • A red highlight area is drawn around areas of the live viewfinder image which have sharp edges, helping you to nail the focus of your shots.
  • Shutter Robot
    • Your friendly shutter automation assistant, with Shutter Robot you can schedule an intervalometer to shoot time-lapse scenes; set up exposure bracketing to shoot HDR; configure a bulb timer to take multi-minute exposures, such as when shooting at night or with filters; or just enable a simple self-timer.

Photo Management

The photo management section of the app gains advanced features for working with your photos, including:

  • On-Camera Image Preview
    • See hi-res previews of the photos on your camera.
  • RAW Support
    • Add support for previewing, copying and sharing the RAW files from your camera. Whether you shoot Canon (CR2), Nikon (NEF), Olympus (ORF), Panasonic (RW2)*, or Sony (ARW) RAW, Cascable can handle them.

    * Some Panasonic and all Fujifilm models are unable to provide RAW images over WiFi.
  • Quick Proof
    • Quickly apply adjustments to your images for exporting or sharing, including Exposure, Highlight and Shadow recovery, White Balance, Contrast, Saturation, Sharpening and Vignette.
  • Filtering & Searching
    • Filter the photos copied to your iPhone or iPad by type, by date range, or use a free text search box to find shots taken using a specific camera make, model, lens, filename and more.
  • External Displays
    • View your photos on the big screen either through AirPlay or by using a HDMI or VGA adapter for your display or TV.

Geotagging

The geotagging section of the app gains advanced features for geotagging your photos, including:

  • Geotag Export
    • When exporting your images, geotags can be applied to generated JPEG images, including when applying Quick Proof settings.
  • Automatically Apply Geotags Based On Location History
    • Cascable can efficiently record your location over time. With Cascable Pro, this location history can be used to automatically apply geotags to any photos taken during the same period. Powerful tools are provided to correct situations when your iOS device and your camera aren't set to the same time or time zone.
  • GPX Export and Import
    • Your Cascable-recorded location history can be exported as a standard GPX file, compatible with a wide range of software. Additionally, if you have a GPX file you'd like to use in Cascable, you can do that too.

Night Mode

Cascable gains an app-wide dark theme, greatly enhancing night and low-light work. By dimming all of Cascable’s user interface your content is king, preserving your night vision so that you can focus on composing and taking nightscapes.
